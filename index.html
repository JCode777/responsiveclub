Massachusetts Astronomy Club

At the Massachusetts Astronomy Club, we have bi-weekly meetings. We have meetings in Boston, Worcester, Amherst, Deerfield and Templeton, MA. The meetings consist of a discussion of different topics, stargazing sessions and astronomy study sessions. Contact your local chapter today to get information about dates and times of meetings!
